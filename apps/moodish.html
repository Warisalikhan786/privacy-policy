Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Moodish is developed by Techi4u. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the app works and what information is or is not collected.

Offline Application

Moodish is designed to work completely offline. Your mood entries are stored locally on your own device. We do not upload your mood history to our servers.

Information We Collect

Moodish does not require account registration. We do not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Date of Birth
  • Mood History on our servers

Your mood records remain stored only on your own device unless you manually delete the application or clear its data.

Advertising

This application may use Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK) to display ads. AdMob may collect information including your Advertising ID, IP address, device information and app usage information in accordance with Google's policies.

Permissions

Moodish requests only the permissions necessary for its functionality. The application does not access your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or location unless a future feature specifically requires it.

Third Party Services

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob (Advertising)

Children's Privacy

Moodish is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
