About Smart Contact Scanner Pro

Smart Contact Scanner Pro is a mobile app that helps individuals and businesses capture contact information in seconds. Instead of typing details by hand, simply scan a business card or document and Smart Contact Scanner Pro extracts the name, phone number, email, and company details automatically — ready to save to your contacts.

How It Works

  • Scan

    Open the app and scan a business card, document, or image containing contact details.

  • Review

    Smart Contact Scanner Pro automatically extracts names, numbers, and emails — review and edit before saving.

  • Save

    Save the contact to your device or, with your permission, directly to your Google Contacts.

  • Manage

    Find every saved contact in your library, and track your scanning activity from the dashboard.
