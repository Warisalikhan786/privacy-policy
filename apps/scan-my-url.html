Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Scan My URL is developed by Techi4u. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information while helping users scan and review links for safety.

Information We Collect

Scan My URL does not require account registration and does not collect personal data from users.

  • No name collection
  • No email collection
  • No phone number collection
  • No location collection
  • No financial or payment information collection

Link Scanning and App Usage

Scan My URL allows users to check links for suspicious or unsafe behavior. Scanned links, history, and preferences are intended to remain on the device unless the app is removed or its data is cleared.

Data Sharing

Scan My URL does not share user data with third parties.

Third Party Services

The app may use standard platform services such as Google Play Services and may connect to link safety services required for URL scanning functionality.

Permissions

Scan My URL may request only the permissions needed for core features such as scanning shared links, opening secure previews, or maintaining app functionality. The app does not request unnecessary sensitive access.

Children's Privacy

Scan My URL is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the app secure. However, users are also responsible for protecting their own devices and using caution when opening links online.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page.
